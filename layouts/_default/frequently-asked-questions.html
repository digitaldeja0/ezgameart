What is EZ Game Art?

A free game asset library available for both personal and commercial projects.

Can I use the art in my game?

Absolutely! You can use the art for all types of projects.

How can I support EZ Game Art?

You can contribute by filling out our volunteer form and sharing your work as either a coder or artist.

How often are new assets released?

They are released every few weeks or whenever new art is contributed by the community.

Can I modify artwork?

Certainly! You have the freedom to modify all work under the Creative Commons license.
